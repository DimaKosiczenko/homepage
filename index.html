    • — The most successful person in life is a person who is happy. — And a happy man is a man who does the work he loves doing the most. He loves his job. Unfortunately not all of us are blessed with jobs that we love doing.

    — A successful man should have faced hurdles and he should’ve been able to accept the hard reality and move on hoping for the best but prepared for the worst.
